Quiz-Explanation

Question 1: What is the commercial capital of Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: Colombo

Question 2: What is the legislative capital city of Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: Sri Jayawardanapura

Question 3: What is the longest river in Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: River Mahaweli

Question 4: What is the currency of Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: Rupee

Question 5: How many districts are there in Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: 25

Question 6: What is the main religion of Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: Buddhism

Question 7: What is the tallest mountain in Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: Piduruthalaga

Question 8: What animal appears on the flag of Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: Lion

Question 9: Name the first Executive President of Sri Lanka?

Correct answer: J.R Jyawardana

Question 10: When did Sri Lanka receive independence?

Correct answer: 1948
